1. Send encrypted messages

Messages enables you to send an encrypted message, it comes with Actoma+ and is based on security chip. Follow any of the ways below to send an encrypted message.

Method 1: Send an encrypted message from Messages. On the main screen of Actoma+, tap Messages to open message list tap a message to open the message windowthen you can send an encrypted message (Make sure you phone is connected to the network).

Method 2: Send an encrypted message from the detailed screen of a contact. On the main screen of Actoma+ tap Contacts select a contact to enter its detailed screentap Send an encrypted message.

Method 3: Send an encrypted message from Calls. On the main screen of Actoma+ tap Calls select a call log to enter its detailed screen tap Send an encrypted message.

2. Make encrypted calls

Calls enables you to make encrypted calls, it comes with Actoma+ and is based on security chip. Follow any of the ways below to make encrypted calls.

Method 1: Make a call from Calls. On the main screen of Actoma+ tap Calls to open call log tap any of the items to make an encrypted call.

Method 2: Make a call from Contacts. On the main screen of Actoma+ tap Contactsselect a contact to enter its detailed screentap Make an encrypted call.

Method 3: Make a call from detailed screen of a call log. On the main screen of Actoma+tap Callsselect a call log to enter its detailed screentap Make an encrypted call.

3. Contacts

Tap Contacts to view all your Actoma+ friends, groups and group address list. You can select a contact to make an encrypted call or send encrypted messages.
